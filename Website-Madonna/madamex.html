MADONNA IS MADAME X

Madame X is the fourteenth studio album by American singer Madonna, released on June 14, 2019, by Interscope Records. The album was creatively influenced by her expatriate life in Lisbon, Portugal, after moving there during the summer of 2017. Madonna co-wrote and co-produced the album with several musicians, including Mirwais, Billboard, Mike Dean and Jason Evigan. It also features guest appearances by Maluma, Quavo, Swae Lee and Anitta.
