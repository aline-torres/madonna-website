About

My name is Aline Torres, and I’m a student from Griffith College. I’m coursing the Postgraduate Diploma in Science in Interactive Digital Media. This project was developed for the Web Authoring lecture. For this project, I planned, designed and built a website to showcase the life/work of someone I admire using HTML and CSS. I used Wikipedia and Youtube for the content of the pages, and the images are all provided by the artist to promote her last album and her work.
